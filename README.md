# StashNest Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

## Information We Collect

StashNest does not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers. All data you create in the app (categories, saved links) is stored locally on your device and optionally synced via Apple iCloud.

When you sign in with Apple, we receive only a unique user identifier provided by Apple. We do not receive your name or email address unless you choose to share them.

## iCloud Sync

If you use iCloud, your data is synced through Apple's iCloud service and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

## Link Metadata

When you save a link, the app fetches the page title and thumbnail image directly from the website on your device. This request is made from your device — we do not proxy or log these requests.

## Third-Party Services

StashNest does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

StashNest is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
