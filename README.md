WordNest Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Information We Collect

WordNest does not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers. All settings and preferences are stored locally on your device.

When you sign in with Apple, we receive only a unique user identifier provided by Apple. We do not receive your name or email address unless you choose to share them.

Word Lists

To provide word suggestions, the app downloads word list files from public GitHub repositories and stores them locally on your device. These requests contain no personal data and are made directly from your device.

In-App Purchases

WordNest offers a one-time lifetime purchase processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not handle or store any payment information. Purchase records are managed by Apple and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

WordNest does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

WordNest is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
